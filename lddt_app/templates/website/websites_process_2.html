STEP 1:
Navigate to LDDT Tracker websites section


STEP 2:
Find the required service in the table


STEP 3: Open Cert-Manager and find for the service, then:
Renew the certificate for the service
Wait for receiving the email with all the certificates


STEP 4: Download the two certificates from the email


STEP 5:
Change the extension of the downloaded file (.pem) to (.cer)


STEP 6:

report All certificates are placed on the libreq server. A separate permission is needed to ssh for it

Move both certificates to AH machine (which has access to libreq server):
ssh from your local laptop to the AH machine: (example) ssh user_name@232.323.323.12
Now, copy both the certificates from your local laptop to the AH machine. To keep things easy, just upload both certificates to the Downloads folder on your AH machine by:
scp *.cer user_name@232.323.323.12:/home/user_name/Downloads

So now, both .cer files are on AH machine in Downloads folder.
